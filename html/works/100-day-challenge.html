100-Day Challenge

I always believe that creativity is a skill — the more you practice it, the more creative you are. So I started my first 100-day design challenge. As a visual thinker, I enjoy vivid color and shaped-based illustration. I believe this 100-day design challenge would be a good opportunity to hone my vector illustration skill.

Each day, I create a vector illustration in Sketch & Figma and post it on my social media account.
